About

CU ConnectAid is a free web-based platform connecting providers, supporters, and recipients of aid in the Champaign-Urbana community. We provide a robust interface to track inventories, hours of operation, donation histories and more in order to better connect donors with donees.

CU ConnectAid was developed by Aneesh Lodhavia, Vishal Sriram, Ryan O'Neall, and Kevin Wong, four undergraduate UIUC students for PygHack 2019. We are a team passionate about using technology to drive social impact and make changes in our community.

Recipient

As a recipient, CU ConnectAid will provide you with relevant information to get the food and supplies you need. On your profile, you'll have an instant view of nearby providers, the ability to search for providers carrying specific items, and give feedback on providers' services.

Provider

As a provider, CU ConnectAid supplies you with the tools you need to better manage your location. Post your address and operating hours, manage your inventory and let recipients know what you're carrying. Keep track of donations from supporters and get digital receipts of your transactions.

Recipient

CU ConnectAid makes supporting your community easier than ever. Easily contact local providers and be confident that your contributions are making an impact with digital receipts of every donation.
